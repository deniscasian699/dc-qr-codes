# Security Policy

## 🔒 Security at DC QR CODES

DC QR CODES is designed with security and privacy as core principles.

### How We Protect Your Data

- **Local Processing**: All QR codes are generated in your browser
- **No Data Collection**: We don't collect or store your QR content
- **Local Storage Only**: History is stored in your browser's localStorage
- **No Account Required**: No personal information needed
- **HTTPS Everywhere**: All connections are encrypted

## 🐛 Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly.

### How to Report

1. **DO NOT** open a public issue
2. **Email** us at [support@deniscasian.com](mailto:support@deniscasian.com)
3. **Include**:
   - Description of the vulnerability
   - Steps to reproduce
   - Potential impact
   - Any suggested fixes

### What to Expect

- **Response**: Within 48 hours
- **Updates**: Regular progress updates
- **Credit**: Acknowledgment if desired (after fix)
- **Timeline**: We aim to fix critical issues within 7 days

## 🛡️ Scope

### In Scope
- qr.deniscasian.com website
- License verification system
- PWA functionality
- Data handling and storage

### Out of Scope
- Third-party services (Firebase, Gumroad, etc.)
- Browser vulnerabilities
- Social engineering attacks

## 📜 Responsible Disclosure

We kindly ask that you:
- Give us reasonable time to fix issues
- Don't access other users' data
- Don't disrupt our services
- Don't publicly disclose before we fix
